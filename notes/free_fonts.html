My Favorite Free Fonts

January 2024 [updated 2026]

There are so many wonderful free fonts in the world. The below are my favorites. They are all so good I sort-of can’t believe they’re free. Truly, we live in a wondrous world.

  • Brill
  • Charter (and XCharter)
  • Classico, a clone of Optima by the original designer
  • eb Garamond
  • ibm Plex Family
  • Inter
  • JetBrains Mono
  • Libre Franklin
  • Libre Baskerville
  • Monaspace
  • P052 (Palladio), a clone of Palatino by the original designer
  • Source Serif 4
  • Utopia (info) also: Erewhon

Some noteable fonts included in macOS Tahoe (You’ll have to explicitly download some of these):

  • Brill
  • Canela
  • Charter
  • Founders Grotesk
  • Graphik
  • Optima
