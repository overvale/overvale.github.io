There are so many wonderful free fonts in the world. The below are my favorites. They are all so good I sort-of can’t believe they’re free. Truly, we live in a wondrous world.

- [Inter](https://rsms.me/inter/)
- [Source Serif 4](http://adobe-fonts.github.io/source-serif/)
- [Brill](https://brill.com/page/BrillFont/brill-typeface)
- [Utopia](https://ghostscript.com/~tor/stuff/fonts/utopia/) ([info](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utopia_(typeface)))
- [IBM Plex](https://www.ibm.com/plex/)
- [JetBrains Mono](https://www.jetbrains.com/lp/mono/)

Some noteable [fonts included in macOS Sonoma](https://support.apple.com/en-us/103203):

- [Brill](https://brill.com/page/BrillFont/brill-typeface)
- [Founders Grotesk](https://klim.co.nz/retail-fonts/founders-grotesk/)
- [Graphik](https://commercialtype.com/catalog/graphik)
- [Produkt](https://commercialtype.com/catalog/produkt)
- [Publico](https://commercialtype.com/catalog/publico)
- [Proxima Nova](https://www.marksimonson.com/fonts/view/proxima-nova)
- [Optima](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Optima)
